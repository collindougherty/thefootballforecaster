Nebraska: Cursed or Consistent?

Last updated on Oct 25, 2021 4 min read Nebraska
Nick Wosika | USA Today Sports

The 2021 season has been cliche Nebraska football under Scott Frost. Frost has raised his total losses in one score games to 17 since becoming the coach at Nebraska, with a record of 5-17 in such contests. Husker fans continue to debate whether or not the team is making progress. Any progress that has been made is hidden underneath an ugly 3-5 record, placing the Huskers last in the Big Ten West. But under the surface, a much more optimistic picture does indeed appear.
